Repair of mechanical watches
One of my hobbies is repairing mechanical watches. It all started when I bought my first old mechanical watch, and after about two weeks I broke the dial in it. Since local watchmakers were unable to repair the damage, I decided to search the Internet and found an advertisement with tips on what I needed and many other parts (about 10 kg in total) The price the seller asked for the tip itself was only slightly less than what he wanted for everything, so I bought it all. And so it all started. Having 10 kg of parts by trial and error (at the beginning there were more errors), I learned to repair watches and many of the parts I bought, I put together a dozen working pieces.
